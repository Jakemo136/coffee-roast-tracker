Screen: Shared Roast (Public)

Read-only view at /share/:token. No auth required. Same layout as roast detail but without edit/delete actions.

Differences from authenticated roast detail:
  • No header/nav — standalone page with minimal branding
  • No edit buttons, no rating interaction, no delete
  • Shows: chart, phase metadata, flavors (read-only pills), notes
  • "Download .kpro" button if profile exists
  • Small "Roast Tracker" wordmark + "Track your own roasts →" CTA at bottom
